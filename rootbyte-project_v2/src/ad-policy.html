Permanently Blocked Categories

Gambling & Betting
Online casinos, sports betting apps, poker sites, slot machine apps, and any game of chance for money. This includes locally-legal PAGCOR-licensed platforms.
Adult Content
Sexually explicit material, adult dating sites, and sexual services of any kind.
Cryptocurrency Scams & High-Risk Crypto Promotions
ICOs, NFT projects promising returns, unregistered exchanges, "guaranteed crypto profits", and any crypto project not registered with the SEC Philippines.
Predatory Lending & Payday Loans
High-interest short-term loans, unlicensed lending apps, and lending with APR over 48% (Philippine SEC maximum for consumer lending).
Political Propaganda & Disinformation
Political campaign ads, partisan political messaging, election interference content, and government propaganda of any kind.
Malware, Scareware & Misleading Software
Fake virus alerts, fake software updates, bundled adware, and any ad designed to trick users into downloading software.
Weight Loss Scams & Miracle Health Claims
Ads making unsubstantiated health claims, diet pills, detox teas, and any product not approved by the Philippine FDA for the advertised claim.
Racial, Religious, or Discriminatory Targeting
Any ad that targets or excludes audiences based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or national origin.
Clickbait & Fake News Disguised as Articles
Ads designed to look like editorial content without clear "Advertisement" labeling, and ads linking to disinformation websites.
